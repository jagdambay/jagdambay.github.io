About Us

Established in 1999, Jagdambay is a leading producer of PET Preforms and Packaged Drinking Water. We consider quality to be paramount to our group values. Our operations are driven by a deep commitment to implementing a robust quality management system that is based on our determination to sustain a culture of operational excellence and meeting the expectation of all stakeholders.
